**Droskar's Crag** is an active volcano located in a mountain range on the edge of [[Darkmoon Vale]]. The mountain consists of two primary peaks known as the Hammer and the Anvil, which reach to an altitude of more than 8 kilometers. Several rivers originate or are fed from glaciers on the Crag.  

The volcano last erupted in 3980 AR in a massive exploding known as the Rending, which destroyed the nearby dwarven cities. At the time the mountain was known as Torag's Crag - it's name was changed after the rending by the surviving dwarves to reflect the hostile nature of the volcano. Since then, it has not shown major activity, though the peaks constantly spew ash and smoke into the air as a constant reminder of the threat beneath that exists beneath the snow-capped mountain.
