# Golarion
The gods did not choose Golarion as the cage for [[Rovagug|The Rough Beast]] by chance - it is a massive world, spanning many continents and realms. Every corner of Golarion teems with wild locals, kingdoms and empires, unique people and cultures, and many adventures to be had.  

Most of the adventures of the Age of Lost Omens take place in a region of Golarion known as the Inner Sea, though some adventurers may visit nearby realms as well.

## The Inner Sea

![500][https://i.postimg.cc/wjmcPDJQ/inner-sea-map.jpg]

Comprising of the continent of Avistan and the northern part of the continent Garund, the region of the Inner Sea gets it's name from the body of water that divides the two continents. Once, this whole region was one large continent, but the shattering of [[History of Golarion#Age of Darkness|Earthfall]] caused the land to splinter and formed the Inner Sea itself. Later, when Aroden raised the Starstone, he built the great city of Absalom around it - everything in the Inner Sea surrounds Absalom, and thus it became known as the "city at the center of the world", an unofficial capital of the region.  

The Inner Sea is further divided into sub-regions, loosely combining kingdoms and realms based on geographic and cultural similarities.

### Saga Lands

Kingdoms, clans, and free cities alike can be found throughout this far-flung stretch of the Inner Sea region, each with its own traditions and legends.
