### Info
A quiet hunter from [[Falcon's Hollow]], kidnapped by the [[Truescale Tribe|Truescale]] kobolds.
- **Ancestry:** Human
- **Gender**: Male
- **Class**: Hunter
- **Traits** Devout, humble, thankful
- **Relationships**
	- [[Terror Attack]] (helpful)
- **Status** alive

### Description
A tall man in his early forties, with dark skin and a patchy beard. His wounds from captivity have not yet fully healed. 

### Biography
Barlus was always somewhat of a recluse, living in a cabin at the edge of town and only occasionally interacting with others other than for selling the meats he hunts. He was also a devout believer of the gods, and specifically [[Erastil]], further making him an outcast in the mostly non-religious Falcon's Hollow.  

During one of his hunting trips, Barlus stayed too long after sundown in the forest. He found his way into [[Jarlbren Trookshavits|Jalbren's]] camp and, exhausted, requested to stay the night, only to be turned away. He instead was forced to camp at the nearby [[Ulizmilla's Hut]], only to be ambushed and captured by kobolds of the [[Truescale Tribe]], who took him back to [[Droskar's Crucible]] as sacrifice for their king's sinister crown.

### Recent History
Thanks to being one of the only townsfolk visiting her church, [[Lady Cirthana Gensar]] felt responsible for Barlus when he disappeared, sending [[Terror Attack]] to find him and kickstarting their adventures in Droskar's Crucible.  

During this time, Barlus was kept prisoner in the kobold warrens until the preparations for the sacrifice ritual were complete. He befriended fellow prisoner Edgrin Galesong, and when a new prisoner arrived, they managed to take advantage of the chaos and escape to the upper level. While Edgrin fought kobolds, Barlus found himself stuck in a dead end, with evil skeletons rising up and haunting him. He fell down to his knees and started preying to Erastil, which seemed to keep the skeletons at bay - until he fell unconcious of exhaustion, at which point the kobolds found him and took him back to his cell.  

Barlus was kept in a bad state, almost ready to be sacrificed before the crown, but Terror Attack mounted a brave rescue mission at the last moment and freed him, carrying the dying hunter back to Cirthana for healing. When he woke, Barlus could not stop thanking the party, and even gifted them his family relic longbow.
