### Info

	- Terror Attack (friendly)

### Description
A woman in her late 40's, Lady Cirthana has tan skin and green eyes, with dark brown hair that becomes grey at the edges. She usually wear priestess robes of red and silver color, with the holy symbol of [[Iomedae]] around her neck.  

### Biography
Lady Cirthana is a true believer in a town of uncaring workers and cynical drunks. She believes it is her mission from her goddess to save the poor souls of Falcon's Hollow, to help guide this quaint town into a brighter future. No one really seems to pay mind to her.  
There are few clerics in Falcon's Hollow. Despite anemic competition from other faiths, and a demonstrated record of care for the town's residents, Cirthana continues to struggle to win converts to her temple. Few locals trust her, and most prefer to trust their medical concerns with Laurel, the local herbalist instead. The Lumber Consortium made itself clear in denouncing the [[Iomedae|Inheritor's]] meddling in the town's affairs, and the locals oblige by avoiding the temple as much as possible.  

Although she tried requesting assistance from her church in effort to reform the town many a times, Iomedaen initiates who come to Cirthana's temple never last long - some leave in frustration from the local populace, deciding to go serve the goddess in someplace more noble and faithful, while other are bribed or intimidated by Consortium agents to leave and never return. And so, Cirthana remains the only cleric of Iomedae in Falcon's Hollow. But she is steadfast in her belief, and continues to work for the benefit of the townfolks, even if it's in small steps, and even if they don't appreciate her for it.

### Recent History
Recently, a hunter by the name of Barlus has gone missing. As one of the few decent believers in town, Lady Cirthana felt a unique obligation to help him. She contracted the Terror Attack adventuring party to track him down.
