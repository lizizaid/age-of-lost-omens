### Description
A young woman in her twenties, Mori has light skin and long, dark hair. She wears simple but elegant clothes. She walks with confidence and has an aura of power about her, causing many to mistake her as some noble lady. She is usually assertive and diplomatic, but often has mood swings and gets frustrated quickly.  
Her familiar, a black cat named Kiki, often sits lazily on her shoulders.

### Biography
Mori is a daughter of a minor noble family, raised in a manor in the city. From youth, Mori's parents pushed her to the scholarly arts, sending her to the best schools and academies to learn. The intelligent young woman always excelled in her studies, but years of stress from her family to always achieve more have marked their strain. Mori became frustrated with her family, arguing with them to no end and slacking in her studies.  

One stressful day, rushing between classes in the academy, Mori's books all fell down in the hallway, and she crawled down to collect them, shaking from frustration. Suddenly, she noticed an elf-like figure helping her collect her notes. The elf set and talked with her in the empty hallway, seemingly very interested in the source of her frustrations. Mori explained the stress her family created for her since childhood, and how, at this moment, she just wished they would all just... disappear. Smiling and chuckling, the figure said, "well, that I can arrange. After all, why not?".  They snapped their fingers and before Mori was able to say anything, they were gone. Over the next couple of days Mori came to a horrifying understanding - the figure made her wish come true. Her family were nowhere to be found. No one, except for her, seemed to remember they even existed. For all intents and purposes, her family was not anymore. She was free - but also terrified.  

Mori has not seen the mysterious figure since. She discovered she now possessed a greater control of primal magic - although she had a rudimentary understanding of magic from her studies, it seems the figure also left her some additional power as some sort of sick joke. She was also now accompanied by a black cat she named Kiki - a magical familiar, and her only connection to the figure. For now, Mori has decided to take on the life of an adventurer to earn her coin, wielding her magical powers to zap her enemies and summon spirit animals. For now, she does not know if she will ever meet her mysterious patron, or her parents, ever again...

### Recent History
Mori joined with two other adventurers, [[Lucas, the halfling|Lucas]] and [[Nachos]], to accept a quest from the Pathfinder Society. They became the original members of Terror Attack. Over their quest, Mori became the unofficial leader of the group, often acting as their mouth and taking care of the other members in an almost motherly manner.
