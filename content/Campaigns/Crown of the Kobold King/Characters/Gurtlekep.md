### Info
An unsuccessful Truescale lookout.
- **Ancestry:** Kobold
- **Gender**: Male
- **Class**: Scout
- **Traits** Fiddly, quick, likes to tinker
- **Relationships**
	- [[Truescale Tribe]] (member)
	- [[Terror Attack]] (hostile)
- **Status** dead

### Description
A short and lanky black-scaled kobold, carrying many small knives on his belt.

### Biography
Not one of the brightest of the [[Truescale Tribe]], Gurtlekep was found to be useful mostly for his quick hands and feet. When they moved into [[Droskar's Crucible]], Gurtlekep was set a lookout at the top of the monastery, and was supposed to raise the alarm if anyone got too close. In reality, the distracted little kobold mostly fiddled with traps and contraptions, often missing adventurers arriving too close.

### Recent History
Gurtlekep was sleeping in his room when [[Nachos]] triggered one of his traps, which alerted the kobold. He tried to fight his way to warn his tribesfolk, but was quickly killed.
