### Info
A happy-go-lucky young swashbuckler, hiding a dark secret.
- **Ancestry:** [[Nephilim, Tiefling|Tiefling]] ([[Human]])
- **Gender**: Male
- **Class**: Swashbuckler
- **Traits** Witty, performative, innocent, golden-hearted
- **Relationships**
	- [[Terror Attack]] (member)
	- Orela (son)
- **Status** alive

### Description
A young man of 19 years old. He has a lithe, acrobatic build, and a cocky smile. The only hint for his devilish origin are a pair of small horns, that he usually hides under his long raven hair and a beaten cloak.  

### Biography
Mattis is the son of Orela, a bard of some renown, that has long settled down. Inspired to do good and make a name for himself just like his mother, Mattis set out to adventure and do good using his naturally sharp tongue.  

Yet when Mattis reached the age of 18, his first [[Devil|devilish]] traits started appearing, now at 19 he is still on a quest to prove himself and do good while searching for the answers as to why he suddenly started developing devilish traits, perhaps his mother hasn't quite shared the whole story with her son...

### Recent History
Mattis was recruited into [[Terror Attack]] as backup during their adventure, and quickly became a core member.  

During his adventures, Mattis felt his devilish inheritance becoming stronger and stronger, threatening to erupt. When returning to [[Falcon's Hollow]] one day, he found that his mother, Orela, has come to visit town, and with her arrived some mysterious figures from her past, especially the [[Devil|devil]] Ophelious - who suggested a bargain with Mattis, offering to remove the tiefling's curse, in return for a future favor.
