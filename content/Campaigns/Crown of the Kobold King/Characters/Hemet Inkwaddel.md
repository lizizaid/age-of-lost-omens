### Description
A stout, older dwarf with coal-black hair wrapped in a ponytail. His bears is slightly charred at the edge.  

### Biography
Hemet is a descendant of a clan of dwarven traders and smith who decided to come down from the mountain cities and look for a simpler life among the humans. Hemet himself has lived in [[Falcon's Hollow]] for most of his adult life, content with setting up shop in the Low Market and crafting simple metal tools for the good townsfolk - sometimes indulging in making weapons and armor for traveling adventurers.  

Unlike most of the town's inhabitants, Hemet does not have a strong opinion about the Lumber Consortium - while he sometime feels bitterness that the town's wealthy will only shop at the High Market, and good quality material never arrives at the Low one, he accepts the Consoritum's rule as lawful and acceptable in these lands.  

### Recent History
Hemet has traded some items with [[Terror Attack]], and while he appreciated their business, they sometimes pestered him too much with bartering attempts.
