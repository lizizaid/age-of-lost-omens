### Info
A wisecracking, massive lizardfolk monk.  
- **Ancestry:** [[Lizardfolk]]
- **Gender**: Male
- **Class**: Monk
- **Traits** Easygoing, flamboyant, chaotic, loves religion and philosophy
- **Relationships**
	- Terror Attack (member)
- **Status** alive

### Description
A large, muscular lizardfolk standing above 2 meters tall. He has light-blue scales with a pale undertone. Usually he wears close to nothing, carrying only his different weapons and religious knicknacks and symbols around his belt.

### Biography
Nachos, the flamboyant, has a different name in lizardfolk tongue. It's very complicated, full of vowels and apostrophes. His friends prefer to just call him Nachos.  
And Nachos prefers it that way.  

Born to a monastic tribe of [[Lizardfolk]] monks, Nachos trained from childhood to hone his body, mind, and soul. His tribesfolk, far removed from other lizardfolk tribes of the area who were simple hunter-gatherers, have followed ancient philosophies passed down the generations for hundreds of years. These ideas centered around a spiritual connection with the world and nature, but most importantly, a wide understanding of the divine. The lizardfolk masters taught a wide range of religious philosophies, the dogmas of different gods and deities, claiming that only an understanding of all divine worldviews may lead to enlightenment. And Nachos, ever the curious philosopher, listened with joy, always looking for the next religion to learn about it's mysteries.  

However, life was not breezy for Nachos at the monastery. Contrary to his cold-blooded brothers and sisters, Nachos was born with a great temperance and a care-free, almost whimsical attitude, that alienated him from other lizardfolk. While Nachos wanted to be free, to see the world and experience all of these religions for himself, his masters required of him discipline and patience. This dissonance made Nachos an outcast in the monastery, and his disobedience sometimes led to greater punishments, ones he does not like to talk about.  

And so, at the day when Nachos realized his life path would lead him elsewhere, he finally decided to leave the monastery. Carrying what little items he owned, Nachos went down from the mountains in search of new friends, new religions, and new adventures.

### Recent History
Nachos managed to find work at the Pathfinder Society adventurers guild, where he teamed up with [[Mori]] and [[Lucas, the halfling]] to take on a quest in the town of [[Falcon's Hollow]]. The three of them were the original members of the Terror Attack adventuring party.
