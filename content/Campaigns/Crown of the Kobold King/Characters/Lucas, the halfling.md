### Info

- **Gender**: Male

- **Traits** Naive, curious, cowardly, full of wanderlust
- **Relationships**
	- Terror Attack (member)
- **Status** dead

### Description
A small, even for his ancestry, halfling of about 18 years old. He has a mess of a red hair, wide eyes, and large ears and front teeth. He wears simple traveling clothes and carries a pretty shortbow and wooden lute lovingly gifted by his parents.

### Biography
Lucas was born to two very loving parents in a small halfling village. Although halflings are usually homely folk, Lucas's parents took this trait to the extreme. As an only child, Lucas was constantly under his folks' supervision and care, and was almost never allowed to leave the house, let alone his village. His parents took care of his education, and made sure their son was well versed in all manner of lores and arts, including history, religion, geography, singing, dancing, and playing the lute. As he grew, he also learned bardic magic, how to control the arcane with his voice. Ever the loyal son, Lucas listened to his parents tales about the scary world outside their home, the many monsters and bandits and traps and diseases waiting to assault the hapless boy the moment he steps foot outside the village. He listened and believed, and stayed home with mom and dad, so that they may ever protect him from the dark, unforgiving world outside.  And so he stayed in his room, and read his books.

But growing among books, one can only go so far before growing an appetite for imagination. He read about the many lands of the world and their interesting peoples and cultures. He read about the many creatures of the world, both the scary and the wondrous. And he read about adventurers, their great stories, and how the experience the world each and every day. He started to think to himself, "what if the world is not really that awful?" Over the years, his curiosity grew, and he developed a wanderlust that sitting at his room all day could not satisfy. He wanted to be just like those heroes from his books. He wanted to see the world with his own eyes, not through words on a page.  

And so, just after his eighteenth birthday, Lucas has gathered what little supplies he thought were relevant for a journey, took up his bow and his lute, and escaped from his house. For the first time in his life, Lucas has existed the safe and warm borders of his village. Alone and confused, but hopeful and excited, he wandered the road for some time, meeting interesting people and building up the courage to interact with them. Miraculously he managed to find his way to the local Pathfinder Society, where he offered up his skills as a bard, looking for his first ever adventure.

### Recent History
For his first adventure, Lucas joined with two other travelers, [[Mori]] and [[Nachos]], and they formed the adventuring party Terror Attack. The Society sent them to the town of Falcon's Hollow to start their quest.  

During their adventure, Lucas started to learn what is means to share the world with other people - how to talk to them, how to fight them, and, most importantly, how to care for them.  

Tragically, Lucas's first adventure was also to be his last, and was cut shortly by the scary dangers his parents warned him about all of these years. During their exploration of Droskar's Crucible, the party were trapped in a magnetic room, where Vargouilles attacked them, and their magical kiss slew Lucas, who's frail body gave way quickly to the fiend's dark attacks. Lucas died with a faint smile on his face, knowing that, for however short his adventure was, he at least got to experience the real world for just a little bit. He was carried back to Falcon's Hollow by his friends and buried there.
