### Info
A cowardly and duplicitous Truescale warrior.
- **Ancestry:** [[Kobold]]
- **Gender**: Male
- **Class**: Scout
- **Traits** Lying, impolite, cowardly
- **Relationships**
	- [[Truescale Tribe]] (member)
	- [[Terror Attack]] (hostile)
- **Status** dead

### Description
A blueish kobold with yellow teeth, broken horns and a crude spear.  

### Biography
Though he tends to claim to be a personal friend of [[King Merlokrep]], Voss is just another ordinary member of the [[Truescale Tribe]]. He survived long due to his fondness of lying and cheating - when he succeeds in his schemes, he can usually be found lazily resting to the side while his fellow kobolds do all the hard and dangerous jobs. Often, though, he fails - which gets him into big trouble. That does not defer Voss from trying the easy way again and again.  

### Recent History
Voss was with a group of kobolds carrying a large piece of obsidian stone for [[Vreggma]] (Voss, of course, was content with standing to the side and barking orders), when [[Terror Attack]] came in, killed his fellows and captured Voss. Deciding to be diplomatic, Voss tried to give the party answers and directions, until they decided he was too annoying and offed the poor fellow.
