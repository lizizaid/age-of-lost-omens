The morning mists part upon the [[River Foam|river's]] shore, as tiny rays of sunshine make their ways between the great Darkwood treetops. A ferry boat lazily rows the final strands towards [[Falcon's Hollow]], docking in the town's small dock.  

"There she is, Falcon's Hollow." the old boatsman tells the travelers parting his ferry. "Home of the desperate and the brave. Are you the former or the latter, friends?" he laughs, as the last three voyagers jump down to the docks - three fresh recruits of the adventuring guild, the Pathfinder Society. A [confident looking young witch](Mori), with a black cat sitting upon her shoulders, followed by two whimsical companions busy bickering with each other: a very [[Lucas, the halfling|young halfling with messy red hair]], looking in awe at the scene around him as if it's the first time he has ever stepped foot in a new town, and a [[Nachos|massive muscular lizardfolk]], seemingly content in teasing the small halfling to no end. "the latter, hopefully," answers the witch, [Mori](Mori). "we are here for a quest by the town's priestess. Hopefully a lot of bravery shall commence - and a lot of rewards." "haha, I admire that spirit!" said the boatsman as he parted with the group. "Good luck, young adventurers, and may the gods watch over you! Yer gonna need it in these dark forests..."  

The party immediately made their way towards the town's small temple of [[Iomedae]], walking through narrow streets and houses of Darkwood logs. [[Lucas, the halfling]], glanced at the town's on-goers with wide eyes. They finally arrived at the church, a small building with Iomedae's holy sword symbol above the front door. Entering the place, the temple seemed to be empty, apart from a woman in priestess clothes working the shrine. "Ah, your'e the Pathfinders I requested? Welcome to Falcon's Hollow." She introduced herself as [[Lady Cirthana Gensar]], the town's only cleric of Iomedae and keeper of the temple. She then explained her mission for the party. A few days ago, a man by the name of [[Barlus Chortun]], a hunter living at the edge of town, has gone missing and was not seen since. Flacon's Hollow is a rough-and-tumble town, she explained, and it was not unusual for locals to simply vanish - thus, the local authorities did not show much interest in the case of Barlus. However, as a cleric of the goddess of justice, Cirthana had to help somehow. Besides, Barlus was deeply religious and one of the few town members who regularly came to pray in the temple, and he came to pray to [[Erastil]] for a successful hunt just before his disappearance - so Cirthana felt a sort of moral obligation to save him, lets the gods chosen let them disappoint their faithful.  

Before continuing, Mori was interested in knowing about other disappearances in the town. "They used to happen all the time." explained Cirthana. The woods are dark and deep, and many beasts, fey creatures, and undead lurk in the shadows, awaiting to snatch wanderers. Just a few years ago, a forest witch named Uzimila kidnapped young children from their beds for her evil rituals. However, recent times have been somewhat peaceful, with no incidents until the case of Barlus. [[Nachos]], distracted with the temple itself and it's religious items, also asked Cirthana to teach him more about her goddess and her ways. He explained he was very interested in understanding and learning about the many gods. Cirthana took compliment from his interest, and promised to discuss Iomedae's philosophy with him, when the time was more appropriate. For now, Cirthana would like them to help find Barlus, hopefully alive. She has little in the ways of gold to offer, but instead she has a magical silver sword in return for saving the poor soul. The last place Barlus was seen is a lumber camp a few hours outside of town, she explained as she marked the place on the adventurers map. The lumberers there are rough and unhelpful, but it's the best place to start their search. "Good luck," said Cirthana as the three left the temple to start their quest, "may Iomedae be your sword and shield."
