### Info
Eccentric shop owner in Midland, obssessed with firearms and black powder  
- **Ancestry:** [[Gnome]]
- **Gender**: Male
- **Class**: Merchant \ Tinkerer
- **Traits** Sharp-minded, friendly, easily distracted
- **Relationships**
- **Status** alive

### Description
A small gnome well into his fifth decade, with messy light brown hair and beard. Can usually be seen covered in oil and powders, obsessively tinkering with some contraption or other.  

### Biography
His love of both the people of [[Korvosa]] and mechanical devices has led Barleton to open [[Korvosa#1. Gadgets and Gizmos|Gadgets and Gizmos]] in [[Korvosa#Midland|Midland]]. Although not a highly technological city, Barleton has found his niche in selling gadgets and mechanical parts to the city's growing industrial factories. After closing hours, Barleton likes to tinker with his own passion projects, or ramble about the newest gun technologies coming from faraway lands to anyone who would listen.  

### Recent History
Barleton first met [[Barton]] when the latter broke into his shop, attempting to steal black powder. He was instantly affixed by his gun, and the two agreed to share knowledge later.
