### Description
Although an old man, with milky-white eyes that suggest blindness, Thousand Bones' vision is better than most men half his age. He has a dark chocolate skin tone and long charcoal black hair, and on his face a white skull is tattooed.  

### Biography
The elder spiritual leader of the Sokan-Quah [[Varisia#Shoanti|Shoanti]] clan, Thousand Bones is somewhat of an anomaly, a bridge between the worlds of the living and the dead. As shaman of the Skull Clan, it's his responsibility to commune with the spirits of the past and lay them to rest, but in recent years, he has also taken upon himself an additional duty: to forge diplomatic ties between his people and those of Korvosa. Although his worldviews have given him much slack with the clan's more willful youth (including his grandson, Gaekhen), who cannot fathom why their weakling patriarch would try and make peace with their traditional enemies, and although the talks have been long and arduous and are bearing almost no results, Thousand Bones continues on patiently with his mission for peace, as he understands living and integrating with the city folk is the only chance the Shoanti people have for surviving and thriving in the modern world.  

### Recent History
Thousand Bones, together with Gaekhen and [[Kebub Urag|Kebub]], went to meet with [[Zenobia Zenderholm]] in an attempt to grant his people working permissions in the city.
