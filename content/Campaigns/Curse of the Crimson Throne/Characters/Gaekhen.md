### Description
A young Shoanti man in his late twenties, with a bald head and white tattoos resembling snaking bones. He is as strong of arm as he is of heart, and prefers to wear an open vest, even when it's cold outside.  

### Biography
As the grandson of the shaman of the Sokan-Quah and one of it's finest warriors, Gaekhen carried much responsibility on his broad shoulders since youth. He constantly finds himself torn between his and his fellow clan youths more traditionalist views, claiming the Shoanti people must continue to live with their own traditions, separate from the rest of the world, to trying and see things his grandfather's way - to accept, and try to live with, the outsiders who have come to inhabit their lands. Still, when putting questions of politics aside, almost everyone at the tribe would agree Kaekhen is a fine man, beloved by his people. He has a strong sense of commodity and would fight and die for any of his fellow warriors, [[Kebub Urag]] chief among them. He is cheerful and likes a good brawl and especially a strong drink - the one thing Gaekhen would agree the pale outsiders have going on for them is a good knack for brewing ale.  

### Recent History
Gaekhen, along with his grandfather [[Thousand Bones]] and [[Kebub Urag|Kebub]] visited Korvosa in an attempt to negotiate work orders for their people in the city, and during that visit he once more experienced the xenophobia and hatred the city folk had for his kind, and became more staunched in his ideals.
