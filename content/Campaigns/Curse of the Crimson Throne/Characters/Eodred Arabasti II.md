### Info
Old and harmless king of [[Korvosa]], liked by the people but also put on the spotlight for his many scandals.  
- **Ancestry:** Human
- **Gender**: Male
- **Class**: Noble
- **Traits** Scandalous, shrewd, popular, womanizer
- **Relationships**
	- Queen Illeosa (Husband)
- **Status** alive

### Description
In his advanced age, old Eodred became quite frail, his long hair total white and his wrinkles showing. When he started to succumb to the Curse of the Crimson Throne he seemed outright sickly, barely managing to speak and mostly drooling. Still, he keeps wearing the latest fashion, dripping with red and gold as befits a ruler of Korvosa.  

### Biography
Eodred Arabasti II is, in fact, not the successor of the first king Eodred - no king of Korvosa has ever had any children, after all. He merely took that name in his youth in hopes of riding the coattails of that popular king's success.  

This somewhat worked for the young noble of house [[Korvosa#Noble Houses|Arabasti]], as he was elected king after the death of queen Domina I. For many years his rule has been steady (if unexceptional), and for the people of Korvosa, steady is the goal. He is known for having a keen skill at dealing with the noble houses of both the city and the surrounding kingdoms, navigating the political landscape with shrewdness, and he negotiated numerous favorable trade agreements with the city's neighbors. In fact, many citizens adored their king so much that they were willing to turn a blind eye to his private life, marked with scandals and excessive spending and - especially - a expensive liking for younger women. It surprised no one, then, that when he finally decided to marry in a relatively old age, it was with a woman a third that age.
