- **Relationships**
	- [[Zim]] (adoptive mother)

### Description
A pretty [[Varisia#Varisians|Varisian]] in her late thirties, Tayce is a tall woman with olive skin, striking auburn hair and green eyes. She is usually seen wearing a Varisian scarf, and her late mother's bronze earrings.   

### Biography
Tayce is the undisputed matriarch of the Soldado family. Since's her husband's unexpected death a few years ago, she has had to raise her five children alone. With little help from her drunkard brother, she juggles between working as a washerwoman for several families and taking care of her family. But Tayce never complains, working hard and fair to earn her pay. She loves her children above anything else and has dedicated her life to raising them right.  

### Recent History
When [[Zim]] was abandoned by his traveling circus and left for his fate in Trail's End, Tayce immediately took him into her family without much questions and gave him a place to stay as if he was one of her own children. She did, however, eventually asked him to help finance the household.
