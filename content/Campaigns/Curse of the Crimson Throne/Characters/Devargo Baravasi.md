### Info

- **Ancestry:** [[Human]]

- **Relationships**

### Description
A large [[Varisia#Varisians|Varisian]] man into his late forties, with a bulky physique and bronze skin. He has piercing brown eyes and a grim outlook. He proudly wears his spider motif on his armor, and his favorite weapons are spiked gauntlets dripping with spider venom. Small arachnids seem to be climbing over his face and body at all times.   

### Biography
Not much is known about the enigmatic "King of Spiders", but he lives to his name in more ways than one. First, he is a true spider of [[Korvosa|Korvosa's]] underworld; His web spanning around many operations in the city, he is sure to always know what is worth knowing about the happenings and the people of Korvosa. But even in the physical sense, he has a direct connection to arachnids - the legends say he can telepathically communicate with the creatures, and the inside of his thieves' den at [[Korvosa#1. Eel's End|Eel's End]] is practically crawling with them and their webs.  

Devargo utilizes his ill reputation and the spook tales told about him to control a powerful criminal operation in Korvosa; His gang have their hands in almost any illicit activity that happens in the city, from conning operations to illegal gambling and drug trade. To his enemies, he is a cold-hearted spider who punishes and kills without mercy, but to his loyal followers and the downtrodden who fills his community, especially fellow Varisians, he is known to be a protective and respectful type of kingpin, who keeps his streets in order and keeps his own people away from harm.   

### Recent History
Following [[Rita Bevenari|Rita's]] advice, [[Zim]] has tried to get a job at Devargo's, Though he was clearly not up for the challenge, Devargo took a liking to the kid and gave him a gig to con people out of their pocket money using his card tricks.
