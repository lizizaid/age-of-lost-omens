### Info
A cheerful street artist with a lucky streak... or an unlucky one
- **Ancestry:** Human
- **Gender**: Female
- **Class**: Bard / Painter
- **Traits** Chatty, art-lover, free spirit, humorous
- **Relationships**
- **Status** alive

### Description
A young [[Korvosa|Korvosan]] in her early twenties, Trinia has a lanky build, fair skin, light blond hair and green eyes. She is almost always marked with bits of paint on her face and clothes.  

### Biography
Growing up in orphanages and in the streets, Trinia never knew her own parents. For most, such childhood spells a doomed future and life as some criminal or drug addict in Korvosa's underworld. Trinia, however, was blessed with a talent and passion for the arts - from childhood she was adept at telling jokes and putting on performances, but she was especially skilled with painting, her dexterous fingers naturally holding pens and brushes. This skill granted her the opportunity to be adopted and raised by a couple of different patrons throughout the years, who have taught her the secrets of the trade, and even the basics of bardic magic, but most importantly, she was raised a fine woman, a freedom-loving individual with an adoration for her city, her art, and life itself.  

When Trinia decided to finally strike on her own, she got a small apartment and earned her bread by becoming a street painter in the parks and squares of Korvosa, and she especially enjoys painting individuals whom she finds striking or unique, capturing both their beauty and imperfections together on her canvas. Content with this simple but rewarding job, Trinia did not expect for her to be one day summoned by the royal court to paint a personal portrait of [[Eodred Arabasti II|King Eodred]] himself, and for a large sum of money indeed...  

### Recent History
Trinia first met [[Barton]] at the docks of [[Korvosa#Midland|Midland]], where she drew his portrait and had lunch with him.
