### Info
A petty, cruel minor crime lord with a fondness for torturing children. 
- **Ancestry:** Human
- **Gender**: Male
- **Class**: Rogue / Crime Lord
- **Traits** Despicable, cruel of heart, bitter, nasty
- **Relationships**
- **Status** alive

### Description
A bent corpse of a man, Gaedren is well into his 70s. He has light, wrinkled skin that is speckled from age, his eyes yellowed and can barely see anymore, his few strands of hair left grey and wet from grease. His left leg has a pronounced limp as he shuffles about. His old skin can no longer stand the chafe of armor more significant than padded armor, and on his rare trips outside, he wears a tattered wide-brimmed sun hat to protect his balding head from sunburn.  

### Biography
A proud and bitter old man, Gaedren Lamm has been a thorn in the side of [[Korvosa]] for decades now. A minor crime lord, one of dozens in the city, Lamm never made too much of a name for himself to get in trouble with the noble houses or make the guard come after him, even as he continued to murder and steal. What sets him apart from other criminals, perhaps, is his especially despicable personality - the only thing that seems to bring a speck of joy to the nasty old man is a good torture session. He takes special joy in terrorizing children, and has a tendancy to abduct orphans and street urchins, making them work for him. Calling them his "little lambs", he forces the children to commit petty crimes to support him and his organization.  

With no real attempts to stop him, Gaedren, his gang of lowlifes, and his abducted lambs have been a plague on Korvosa's poorer neighborhoods for years, stealing from the downrotten, leeching off drug addicts, and kidnapping or killing whenever their hearts desire so.
