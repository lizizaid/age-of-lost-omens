### Info

- **Ancestry:** Human

- **Class**: Commoner
- **Traits** Cheerful, intrusive, meddlesome, always moving
- **Relationships**
	- [[Devargo Baravasi]] (Worker)

### Description
A beautiful [[Varisia#Varisians|Varisian]] woman in her early twenties, with a large puff of curly raven black hair that always seems to get everywhere. She has a bronze skin tone, and like to wear striking red and lots of jewelry, even if it's fake or stolen.  

### Biography
Rita is more of a child of [[Korvosa#Trail's End|Trail's End]] than of any particular family there - she known everyone, and everyone knows her. She has a certain reputation in the neighborhood, as she is known for sticking her nose in everyone's and anyone's affairs, always seeming to appear at the wrong time in the wrong place, always chiming in when folks are having their most private conversations. If you want to hear the latest gossip about anyone in town, you go to Rita. People adore her yet despise her guts at the same time the way only a child living in such a closed-knit community could be felt toward. Still that adoration takes over completely, and her sins washed away, when at nights she dances in the taverns and pubs of Trail's End. If there's one thing Rita loves more than gossip, it's music, and she dances her people's traditional steps so beautifully she always inspires the crowd into a sort of hypnotized trance, and when the song is over they all cheer and buy her a round of ale.  

Rita is also closely connected to [[Devargo Baravasi]],  and have done odd jobs for the criminal for years now in order to earn her pay. Like all of the young Varisians he takes under his wing, Devargo has developed a soft spot of Rita hidden in his cold shriveled heart, and everyone knows that if you dare hurt her in any way, you'll soon fin Devargo's men knocking on your door.  
### Recent History
Rita was the one to first suggest to [[Zim]] to try and find a job at Devargo's.
