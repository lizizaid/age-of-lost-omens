Elusive, flighty, and whimsical, **sprites** are one of the most common and known types of fairies. All fairies share a diminutive size, a connection to magic, and a love of pranks and adventure.  

### Description
Sprites exist in many shapes and colors, but most are tiny, no bigger than 25 centimeters. They have large wings and sharp, fey ears. Many sprites like to wear leaves and flowers as dresses, and use small needles and twigs as weapons.  

### Ecology
As fey creatures, sprites can be born in either the Feyrealm or the material world, though due to their curios natures they tend to move between places and worlds frequently. Some sprites in the material world are born without their wings, and grow them through experience and connection to the world.  

### Society
Sprites are exuberant pranksters, and enjoy freedom, music and laughter the most. Their jokes, although annoying, are rarely malicious or dangerous and, due to their skittish behavior, rarely long-lasting. They are also fierce defenders of nature and magic, though not all recipients of their protection wand or need it.  

Sprites tend to congregate into families and villages, and not only welcome but celebrate the arrival of new sprites to their communities. They prefer to live in forest canopies, natural caves, or places linked with the Feyrealm.  

### Sprite types
While human scholars try to categorize sprites into types by characteristics, sprites themselves find this tendency amusing, and see all sprites as one big family.  

Common sprites, also known as **firefly sprites**, have the appearance of a colorful humanoid with wings. Other sprite types include:
- Atomie
- Pixie
