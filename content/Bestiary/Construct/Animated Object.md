Granted a semblance of life through use of rituals or other strange magics, **animated objects** take many forms and serve a variety of functions, though they commonly serve as guardians for the homesteads of magic users/

### Description
All manner of objects can be animates using magic. Common household items such as brooms or silverware are the cheapest options, while animated armor and weapons prove more durable and dangerous. Statues are also sometimes animates as guardians. The range of animated objects is great, with some unique items existing such as self-shooting trebuchets or entire animated ships.

### Ecology
An object can be animated using magic, either from a spellcaster or a wild magic surge. Spellcasters especially like using such objects to automate menial tasks for them, such as cleaning or cooking, or to keep as guards against would-be thieves.
