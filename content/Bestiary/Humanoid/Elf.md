Magical, mystical, and mysterious, **Elves** are among the most ancient peoples of [[The World of Golarion|Golarion]]. Their long lives and storied history grants them a unique perspective on life, which they see as an endless opportunity for learning, loving, and exploring. 

### Description
Elves are on average taller and more slender than [[Human|humans]], with relatively fragile, elongated features and a natural grace. They have colored pupils that fill most or all of their wide, rounded eyes in a manner unlike the other ancestries of Golarion. Their colouration varies largely based on their environment; for instance, the skin of an elf who has long dwelled in deserts might be golden-hued. The same is often true about their hair, eyes, and other physical features. Elven ears are generally between six to nine inches in length and point backwards, angling upwards slightly to end usually parallel to the crown of the head.  

Elves tend to reach physical adulthood by their 20th year but are considered mature by their kin as they reach 100. The average elf's lifespan is approximately 600 years.

### Society
Elves tend to be patient and insightful, with a strong intellectual curiosity and sense of wonder. They tend to value freedom, privacy, individualism, and flexibility, allowing themselves many pursuits before finding a well-suited passion. Their long lives grants them world views that makes other ancestries view them as haughty but also mysterious and alluring; While a human artist might spend a few months on a painting, an elven artist might work for years or even centuries on a single masterpiece, instilling in it the cumulative beauty and understanding of seeing the world change for years at a time.  

Elves tend to live in harmony with nature and magic. From small treetop villages to magnificent magical ivory cities, their settlements are filled with beauty, art, song and laughter. Their graceful builds makes elves natural archers and rangers, while their innate connection to the world around them presents in elven wizards and druids.
