Sons and daughters of the [[Cosmology#Lower Planes|Lower Planes]], **Tieflings** are [[Nephilim]] who trace their heritage to  [[Demon|Demons]], [[Devil|Devils]], or other Fiends. They are often distrusted and feared for their evil lineage.

### Description
Most tieflings assume the look of a standard member of their ancestry, with their Fiendish heritage showing in subtle ways: long and sharp fangs, cat-like eyes, a small pair of horns. Some possess a hellish red skin tone, a demonic tail or bestial hooves; Others are marked by more hideous features such as writhing tentacles, full body mutilations, or bulges beneath the skin that move and crawl like insects.  

### Ecology
Tieflings are born from mortal interactions with Fiends. As mortal capacity for sin and evil is endless, tieflings are one of the most common types of Nephilim - some are created when a forefather is tempted by a demon of lust or a devil's silver tongue, while others are from a long family line cursed by the fiendish dealings made by an ancestor many generations ago.  

### Society
Tieflings are rarely welcomed in humanoid societies due to their fiendish descent and out-worldly appearance. As such, many tieflings live in ghettos and other undesirable areas of settlements. They face a significant amount of prejudice from other races, who might view them as seeds of evil, monsters, and lingering curses upon the world. Despite this, tiefling has the power to choose their fate like any other mortal, and while some embrace their dark heritage and side with Fiendish powers, many other reject their evil predilections and become adventurers and heroes in their own rights.
