Optimistic, cheerful, and driven by powerful wanderlust, **halflings** make up for their short stature with an abundance of bravado. At once excitable and easygoing, halflings are the best kind of opportunists, and their passions favor joy over violence. While their curiosity sometimes drives them toward adventure, halflings also carry strong ties to house and home. 

### Description
Halflings are small humanoids, standing at an average of less than one meter tall. Their proportions are mostly similar to humans, usually with a pleasant round face and large round ears. Halflings have large feet with tough soles and growths of hair on the top, allowing some to walk bare-footed.  
Halflings have lifespans just a tad longer than most humans, reaching adulthood at about the same time as them as living an average of 120 years.

### Personality
Halflings are generally easy-going yet excitable, staid yet frantic if roused. Their greatest advantage is ironically their perceived physical weakness, as halflings know that they will usually be underestimated by the larger folk. Halflings are loyal to their friends and their family, but they aren't afraid to do what needs to be done in order to survive. 

### Society
Despite their jovial and friendly nature, halflings don't usually tend to congregate. They have few cultural centers in the Inner Sea region, and they instead tend to weave themselves throughout the societies of the world. Halflings eke out whatever living they can manage, many performing menial labor or holding simple service jobs. Some halflings reject city life, instead turning to the open road and traveling from place to place in search of fortune and fame. These nomadic halflings often travel in small groups, sharing hardships and simple pleasures among close friends and family.
