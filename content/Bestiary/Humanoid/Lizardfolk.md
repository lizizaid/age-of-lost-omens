**Lizardfolk**, also known as **Iruxis** in their ancient tongue, are reptilian humanoids who dwell in the Inner Sea's marshes and swamps. Although their ancestry is ancient far beyond most other races of the region, at this age they are mostly a tribal people, hunting and gathering at the margins of the world, away from other civilizations.

### Description
Lizardfolk are usually tall and muscular, with scales of green, blue or black, though other colors do exist. They have long, powerful tails, and strong jaws and claws. They are primarily swamp dwellers, and are good swimmers. In the water, they propel themselves wil their tails in a manner reminiscent of an alligator.  
Young lizardfolk undergo puberty at 10 years of age, and can live to about 60-80 years.

### Ecology
Lizardfolk usually live in small tribes in damp marshes and swamps, although they can be found in any warm climate, such as jungles and deserts. Lizardfolk are cold-blooded, and cannot regulate their body temperature internally. As such, they are primarily diurnal and become sluggish during the night. They are also intolerant of low ambient temperatures, leading to their preference for warm and temperate environments.

### Society
Lizardfolk of the Inner Sea can trace their lineage to great, ancient civilizations, who dominated the land long before the first [[Human]] or Elf kingdoms came to be. Although at this age they have regressed to basic tribal existence, their long and celebrated history is kept by tribe elders via song and dance, and Iruxi have tradition of religion, philosophy and astrology uncommon with other tribal humanoids.  

Lizardfolk tend to be isolationist, cold-blooded people. They live off the land and take what they need, usually sharing with the tribe and having no concept of personal belongings. A tribe is led by a patriarch or a matriarch, with tribe elders as shamans or bards being the spiritual leaders of the people. Lizardfolk have a natural connection with other reptiles, often domesticating giant lizards to hunt among them.  

Some legends speak of lizardfolk nations deeper in the Mwangi jungles, small remnants of their once great empires, with golden pyramid temples and warriors riding on the back of dinosaurs.
