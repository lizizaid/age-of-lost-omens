**Wolves** are predatory canines that hunt in packs. Their powerful jaws and predator senses make them dangerous opponents out in the wilderness, but their loyalty to the pack makes them a favored hunting companion and a symbol of prowess among many cultures.

### Description
Wolves are large, furred canines, standing at about 60 centimeters at shoulder height. Their coats range widely in color from grey to brown and even black or white among some species. Their most well known feature is their mournful howl, with which they communicate to pack members.

### Ecology
Wolves mostly live in packs, led by a mated pair. They are extremely territorial, and can be found in most lands, from forest and tundra to plains and desert.  
Some powerful creatures with control over nature, such as Vampires, can call upon wolves to aid them in combat.
