In the world of Golarion, religion is a key aspect in any mortal's life. The multitude of gods and divinities of the universe vow to shape the world, each with their own dogmas and a vision on how the world should look like - whether it should be healed, molded, or destroyed. The gods are ideas manifested, incredible divine power coalesced into beings beyond mortal understanding, but they are powerless without their followers - whether they be commoners muttering prayers under their breath, or divine warriors on the front lines of cosmic wars between angels and demons. Through their followers, a deity can shape the world's destiny.
