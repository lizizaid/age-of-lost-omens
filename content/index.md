*The wheel of time turns, and ages come and pass, leaving memories that become legend. Legend fades to myth, and even myth is long forgotten when the age that gave it birth comes again. In one age, called the Age of Lost Omens by some, an age yet to come, an age long past, four adventurers arrived in the Varisian town of Sandpoint. Their adventure was not the beginning. There are neither beginnings nor endings to the turning of the wheel of time.*  

*But it was a beginning.*  


---

The Inner Sea region has seen many ages come and go; from the Age of Darkness, when the cataclysmic Earthfall covered the sky with ash for a thousand years, to the Age of Destiny, when great empires rose and clashed over the future of the land. The most prominent figure throughout these ages has always been Aroden, the last Azlanti, god of humanity. He led the broken remnants of humanity through the Age of Darkness, he  became the patron of great empires, he raised the Starstone from the sea and ascended to godhood. He was prophesied to be the chosen one, to usher in a new age of peace and prosperity to the world. And so, on the day the augurs and prophets foresaw Aroden's return to Golarion from his long voyage, all of the Inner Sea's people stood in excitement and looked at the sky, waiting for the chosen one's salvation to come to them.  

On that day, Aroden died.  

His divine death had catastrophic effects on the Inner Sea. Clerics and paladins of Aroden failed to receive power from their god. Great storms tore open the sky and sea, flooding entire nations. Kingdoms erupted into succession wars. The borders between realities shattered, allowing demons and other monsters to slip into the world and massacre many. And, most importantly, the power of  prophecy failed. No living soul could gaze into the future again. No mortal could know their destiny. This was the Age of Lost Omens.  

A hundred years have passed since Aroden's demise, and the Inner Sea has managed to return to a time of relative peace. Yet storm clouds gather, and dark times loom once more on the horizon. Ancient foes seem to slowly rise from their slumber, attempting to claim their dominion over the lands yet again, while new villains gain power in the shadows, awaiting an opportunity to strike an unsuspecting world. The people of the Inner Sea cannot rely upon prophecy to save them from these forces. There cannot be a chosen one anymore.  

This age calls for heroes to take up arms and rise to the occasion!


# Introduction
Welcome to the Age of Lost Omens!  

This is a mega-campaign for Pathfinder 2nd edition, containing the stories of all campaigns our groups shall run in the world of Golarion, in the Inner Sea region. In this site you can find campaign notes from each game, as well as lore notes about the history and geography of the world, the people and monsters you have encountered, and more. As you explore the world, more lore will be revealed.  

As always, thank you for playing with me!
