The **Harrowing** sub-system allows characters who have been infused with great Harrow powers to take control of their own fates and vastly increase their powers as a result.  

# Rules
At the start of each book, the players will receive a Harrowing. The latest Harrowing for each character can be found at the bottom of this page, [[Harrowing#Last readings|here]].  

All players start with 1 Harrow Point. Each character can then try to achieve Harrow Points based on their reading, up to 9 points per book. To earn a Harrow Point for a card that has been opened, a character must complete a task or achievement relevant to the alignment of that card in their reading. For example, if a character got the *Paladin* card opened in the Lawful Good alignment, and they complete the achievement noted for The Paladin under Lawful Good ("Bring justice to the wicked"), they gain 1 Harrow Point. For neutral alignment cards, the character can choose which achievement to pursue - neutral good can take the lawful good or chaotic good achievements, neutral evil can take the lawful evil or chaotic evil achievements, and neutral can attempt any achievement. If you complete an achievement corresponding to the card opened in your Choosing, gain 3 Harrow Points instead. After the next reading is done, any achievement not finished can no longer be completed, and the players receive a new set of achievements.  

Players can spend their Harrow Points at any time on rewards or upgrades. See the [[Harrowing#Rewards|Rewards]] section below. Rewards can be either one-time spends, costing 1 Harrow Point, or a permanent upgrade called an **Investiture**, costing 5 Harrow Points. Investitures are powerful upgrades, one for each of the Harrow cards. A character that permanently dies loses all of it's Investitures.

# Rewards
## One time spends
- Make a single roll become one step of success higher, as if a hero point was used.
- Force the GM to re-roll a single roll.

## Investiture
**The Paladin**
- You gain the *Shield Block* general feat. If you already have that feat, you gain an additional reaction at the start of your turn that can be used only to shield block.
- Gain the following reaction: <- **Frequency** once per day; **Trigger** You fail or critically fail a saving throw; **Effect** Adjust the result of your saving throw up by one degree of success.  
**The Keep**
- You gain a +1 bonus to saving throws against Fear effects. This bonus increases to +2 at level 11, and +3 at level 17.
- Starting at level 11, whenever you would become Frightened, reduce the condition value by 1.
- Starting at level 11, you gain the following action: >> envision, Interact; **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You cast [_magnificent mansion_](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=183). The level of the spell is one-half your level, rounded down (minimum 7th level), and the interior of the mansion appears to be the inside of a resplendent keep made of stone.  
**The Big Sky**
- You gain a +1 bonus to Perception checks made while flying, and to Escape. This bonus increases to +2 at level 11, and +3 at level 17.
- You gain the following action: >> envision; **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** For 10 minutes, you and up to four creatures you touch ignore [difficult terrain](https://2e.aonprd.com/Rules.aspx?ID=453) and effects that would give a circumstance penalty to Speed. If you are at least 17th level, the targets also ignore greater difficult terrain.  
**The Forge**
- You gain a +1 bonus to Crafting checks made to Craft. This bonus increases to +2 at level 11, and +3 at level 17.
- You gain the following action: >> envision, Interact; **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You pass your hand over an adjacent [broken](https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=2) (but not destroyed) object ([magical](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=103) or otherwise) of up to 4 Bulk, and restore the object to its full Hit Point total, removing the broken condition in the process.

You gain a +1 status bonus to your check. This bonus increases to +2 if you're at least 11th level, and +3 if your'e at least 17th level.

- You gain the following action: >> command, envision; **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** The card calls forth a mob of shadowy figures who batter and hinder your enemies. Target a 20-foot burst at any point on solid ground with 120 feet, and the mob of figures rises up from the ground, persisting for 1 minute. Make an [unarmed](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=199) Strike against the Fortitude DC of any number of creatures in this burst (you can choose to not attack some creatures in the area if you wish). Any creature you succeed against is [grabbed](https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=20) and takes 3d6 bludgeoning damage. It's AC is equal to your class DC, and it's destroyed if it takes 12 or more damage. Even if destroyed, additional figures continue to rise up in the area until the effect's duration ends. You can Dismiss this effect. If you are at least 15th level, you can use a two-action activity, which has the [concentrate](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=32) trait, to move the burst up to 30 feet, which causes any currently grabbed creatures to be released and left behind.
