### Info
- **Level:**
	- 1 (Lesser)
	- 3 (Moderate)
	- 11 (Greater)
	- 17 (Major)
- **Rarity**: Common
- **Type**: Alchemical Elixir
- **Cost**: 
	- 4 gp (Lesser)
	- 12 gp (Moderate)
	- 300 gp (Greater)
	- 3,000 gp (Major)

### Description
After you drink this thick mutagen, your body becomes sturdy and ponderous.  

### Usage
**Benefit** You gain an item bonus to Fortitude saves and the listed number of temporary Hit Points. Whenever you're at maximum Hit Points for at least 1 minute, you regain the temporary Hit Points.  
**Drawback** You take a –2 penalty to Will saves, Perception checks, and initiative rolls.  

- (Lesser) The bonus is +1, you gain 5 temporary Hit Points, and the duration is 1 minute.
- (Moderate) The bonus is +2, you gain 10 temporary Hit Points, and the duration is 10 minutes.
- (Greater) The bonus is +3, you gain 30 temporary Hit Points, and the duration is 1 hour. When you roll a success on a Fortitude save, you get a critical success instead.
- (Major) The bonus is +4, you gain 45 temporary Hit Points, and the duration is 1 hour. When you roll a success on a Fortitude save, you get a critical success instead, and your critical failures on Fortitude saves become failures instead.
