### Description
A vial of ruby-red liquid, a healing potion imparts a tingling sensation as the drinker's wounds heal rapidly.  

### Usage
- (Minor) You restore 1d8 HP.
- (Lesser) You restore 2d8+5 HP.
- (Moderate) You restore 3d8+10 HP.
- (Greater) You restore 6d8+20 HP.
- (Major) You restore 8d8+30 HP.
