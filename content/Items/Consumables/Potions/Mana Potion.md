### Description
This crystal-blue potion grants spellcasters some additional magical stamina, restoring their spellcasting capabilities.  

### Usage
**Activate** > (manipulate); You restore used spell slots, with an amount equal to at most the rank of the potion. For example, a 3rd rank mana potion can restore one 3rd rank spell slot, one 2nd rank and one 1st rank spell slots, or three 1st rank spell slots.
