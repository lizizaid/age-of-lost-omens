### Info
- **Level:** 5
- **Rarity**: Uncommon
- **Type**: Consumable
- **Cost**: 25 GP

### Description
These magical candies are made by extremely chaotic wizards. Each popper has a random flavor. When eaten, the chaotic magic within the popper bursts, causing a random unknown magical effect. There are more than 10,000 popper flavors, so beware!  

### Usage
**Activate** > (manipulate); You eat the popper and produce a random magical effect. A popper’s effect can be removed with a *dispel magic* spell or similar magic.
