### Info
- **Level:** 4
- **Rarity**: Common
- **Type**: Talisman
- **Cost**: 40 GP

### Description
Dark smoke seems to writhe within this obsidian gem, which empowers your weapon with frightening energies.  

### Usage
**Usage** affixed to a weapon; **Duration** 1 minute; For the duration, you gain the following action:  

**Activate** >> (concentrate) Make a strike. If you hit and deal damage, the target is frightened 1, or frightened 2 on a critical hit. If you have the Intimidating Strike feat, increase the frightened condition value caused by the Strike to 2, or 3 on a critical hit.
