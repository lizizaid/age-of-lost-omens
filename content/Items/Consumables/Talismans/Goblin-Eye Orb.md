### Info
- **Level:** 5
- **Rarity**: Common
- **Type**: Talisman
- **Cost**: 48 GP

### Description
This colorful marble with a painted red eye dangles from a leather throng wrapped around the affixed weapon.  

### Usage
**Usage** affixed to a range weapon; **Duration** 1 minute; **Requirements** you are at least Expert in the affixed weapon. For the duration, when you make a ranged attack, you do not need to attempt a flat check due the enemy being Concealed or Hidden from you.
