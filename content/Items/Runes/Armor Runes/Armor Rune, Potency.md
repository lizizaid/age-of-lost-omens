	- 200,000 gp (+4)
	- 5,000,000 gp (+5)

### Description
This basic enchantment grants the armor magical wards that deflect attacks.    
### Usage
- (+1) The armor's bonus to AC increases by +1, and the armor can be etched with one property rune.
- (+2) The armor's bonus to AC increases by +2, and the armor can be etched with two property runes.
- (+3) The armor's bonus to AC increases by +3, and the armor can be etched with three property runes.
- (+4) The armor's bonus to AC increases by +4, and the armor can be etched with four property runes.
- (+5) The armor's bonus to AC increases by +5, and the armor can be etched with five property runes. +5 Potency Runes are very rare, and can usually only be found on the most legendary of armor sets or on Artifacts.
