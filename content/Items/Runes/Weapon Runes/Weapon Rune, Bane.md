### Info
- **Level:** 
	- 4 (regular)
	- 12 (greater)
	- 19 (devastating) 
- **Rarity**: Uncommon
- **Type**: Weapon Rune
- **Cost**: 
	- 100 GP (regular)
	- 5,000 GP (greater)
	- 150,000 GP (devastating)

### Description
This rune grants the enchanted weapon a magical hatred toward one type of creatures, drawing more blood when striking such creatures.   

### Usage
Each *bane* rune is crafted for a specific creature trait, such as Abberation, Animal, Fey or Dragon. For broader creature types, such as Humanoid, Fiend, or Undead, specific *bane* runes might be crafted - such as *Elf bane* or *Zombie bane* runes. The enchanted weapon deals additional weapon of it's damage type to creatures with the chosen trait.  
- (regular) The weapon deals an additional 1d6 damage.
- (greater) The weapon deals an additional 2d6 damage.
- (devastating) The weapon deals an additional 3d6 damage. On a critical hit, it deals 3d12 damage instead.
