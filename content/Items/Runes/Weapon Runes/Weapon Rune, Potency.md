### Description
The Potency Rune is a basic enchantment, that allows the weapon it is etched on to strike true.  
### Usage
- (+1) Attack rolls with this item gain a +1 item bonus, and the weapon can be etched with one property rune.
- (+2) The bonus to attack rolls increases to +2, and the weapon can be etched with two property runes.
- (+3) The bonus to attack rolls increases to +3, and the weapon can be etched with three property runes.
- (+4) The bonus to attack rolls increases to +4, and the weapon can be etched with four property runes.
- (+5) The bonus to attack rolls increases to +5, and the weapon can be etched with five property runes. +5 Potency Runes are very rare, and can usually only be found on the most legendary of magical weapons or on Artifacts.
