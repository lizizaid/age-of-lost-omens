### Description
Magical enhancements make your spellcasting more focused and powerful.  

### Usage
- (+1) Your spellcasting DC and attack bonus both gain +1 item bonus, and the spellcasting focus can be etched with one property rune.
- (+2) The bonus increases to +2, and the spellcasting focus can be etched with two property runes.
- (+3) The bonus increases to +3, and the spellcasting focus can be etched with three property runes.
- (+4) The bonus increases to +4, and the spellcasting focus can be etched with four property runes.
- (+5) The bonus increases to +5, and the spellcasting focus can be etched with five property runes. +5 Potency Runes are very rare, and can usually only be found on the most legendary of spellcasting foci or on Artifacts.
