### Info
- **Level:** 
	- 5 (Regular)
	- 10 (Greater)
	- 15 (Major)
- **Rarity**: Common, Uncommon (Greater), Rare (Major)
- **Type**: Spellcasting Rune
- **Cost**: 
	- 300 GP (Regular)
	- 900 GP (Greater)
	- 10,900 GP (Major)

### Description
This rune makes plants you create with your magic sharp with dozens of small thorny needles.  

### Usage
- (Regular) When you cast a non-cantrip spell with the Plant, Wood, or Fungus traits, any enemy affected by the spell takes an additional 1d6 piercing damage. If the creature is critically hit or critically fails it's saving throw, it also takes 2 persistent bleed damage.
- (Greater) The additional piercing damage increases to 2d6, and the persistent bleed damage increases to 4.
- (Major) The additional piercing damage increases to 3d6, and the persistent bleed damage increases to 6.
