### Info
- **Level:** 10
- **Rarity**: Unique
- **Type**: Held Item
- **Cost**: -

### Description
Hand-painted images decorate this harrow deck, and the cards frames are gilded in silver so that they sparkle and ash under lighting. Despite the worn condition of the card backs, the images on the faces are so vibrant they seem to move when viewed out of the corner of the eye. The deck handles with surprising ease, almost shuffling itself. A bent, torn, or lost card always seems to mend itself or reappear when no one is looking.

These features are subtle manifestations of the spirit that haunts the cards. In life, [[Zellara Esmeranda|Zellara]] lived by this deck, and in death, she has become the deck. Her purpose now is to defend and protect the city of [[Korvosa]], her home in life and death. To that end, Zellara can create a Harrow bond with any PC to whom she performed a Harrow reading.

### Usage
Any PC bonded with Zellara gains access to the [[Harrowing]] subsystem.  

In addition, any PC bonded with Zellara's Harrow deck gains the following features. Each daily abillity granted by the deck can be used by one of the PCs, after which they cannot be used by any PC until the next day.  

**Gifts**: You channel the Harrow bond to create a powerful boon from Zellara's spirit. Zellara can create an amount of gifts equal to the amount of Harrow readings she performed, up to 6 in total per day. For each such level, any PC can choose to use Zellara's Life or Luck gifts; afterwards, a gift for that level can only be used again the next day. If a gift does not have a Duration, it's Duration is instead 1 day.  

1st: [Bestow life](https://2e.aonprd.com/Relics.aspx?ID=166) or [Beginner's luck](https://2e.aonprd.com/Relics.aspx?ID=97)  

2nd: [Healing wave](https://2e.aonprd.com/Relics.aspx?ID=167) or [Swap luck](https://2e.aonprd.com/Relics.aspx?ID=98)  

3rd: [Overflowing life](https://2e.aonprd.com/Relics.aspx?ID=168) or [Whammy](https://2e.aonprd.com/Relics.aspx?ID=99)  

4th: [Inextinguishable](https://2e.aonprd.com/Relics.aspx?ID=169) or [Clean luck](https://2e.aonprd.com/Relics.aspx?ID=100)  

5th: [Vitality siphon](https://2e.aonprd.com/Relics.aspx?ID=170) or [Steal luck](https://2e.aonprd.com/Relics.aspx?ID=101)  

6th: [Life everlasting](https://2e.aonprd.com/Relics.aspx?ID=171) or [Against all odds](https://2e.aonprd.com/Relics.aspx?ID=102)  
  
**Activate** > **Frequency** 3 times per day; **Effect** An image of Zellara can summoned from the deck in order to gain information about Korvosa. Zellara's spirit has a strong connection to the city and her people, and so can always make a Society or Korvosa Lore check with a modifier equal to the highest PC Society modifier.  

**Activate** 1 minute **Frequency** Once per day; **Effect** You hold a card from the deck up to your head while focusing on a magic item in your hand. After 1 minute, you automatically critically succeed on an Identify Magic attempt for the item. This ability can only be used for items up to your Level +2.
