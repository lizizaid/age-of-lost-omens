### Info
- **Level:** 5
- **Rarity**: Common
- **Type**: Gloves
- **Cost**: 150 GP

### Description
Glowing runes of divine light cover these handwraps.  

### Usage
Restorative Handwraps are +1 Striking [[Handwraps of Mighty Blows]]. You deal an additional 2 Vitality damage on Strikes with these handwraps. You also gain the following reaction:  
<- **Frequency** once per 10 minutes; **Trigger** you critically hit an enemy with the Restorative Handwraps; The radiant runes glow, restoring you 2d8+4 HP.
