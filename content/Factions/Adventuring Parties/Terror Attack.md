## Description
Terror Attack is an adventuring party based in the Darkmoon Vale, made of a couple of ragtag mercenaries working together. 

## History
The original three members of the group came together in a Pathfinder Society lodge not far from Darkmoon Vale, and came together to take a contract by [[Lady Cirthana Gensar]], a priestess from the town of [[Falcon's Hollow]].
