# The Cosmos
The Eternal Sea. The Worlds Without Number. The Great Beyond. A multiverse of infinite possibilities. For eons, mortal scholars have attempted to unravel a glimmer of the secrets of the **Cosmos** - secrets of magic, of the gods, and of creation itself.  

Though it's truest nature could never be truly understood by mortal minds, cosmologists today have refined a model that explains how our universe is built. It is called **The Great Cosmic Wheel**. This model claims that the cosmos, just as any creature living in it, is built of four parts. The **Body**, the physical manifestation of the universe. The **Mind**, the realm of thoughts and ideas. The **Soul**, the divine spirituality of concepts and emotions. Finally, the **Spirit**, the life force that connects and animates the universe altogether. Each of these four components are represented by **Planes**, the cosmic manifestation of universal force.  

### Essences
At their very essence, all planes are built from **Quintessence**, the most basic blocks of the cosmos; Each plane builds an **Essence** around it's Quintessence that allows it to metaphysically "shed" over the universe. Thus, a mortal being living in a physical Plane might, for example, feel love, anger or fear due to the Essence of the soul Planes feeding it's soul. At the same time, those creatures who make the soul Planes their home have a materialized body and walk on solid ground due the physical Essence asserting the universe has a physical manifestation. Essences are thus the blocks of not only their relevant planes and their inhabitants, but are spread among the entire cosmos to make it whole.  

Though the universe is perfectly made and balanced between the four components, it is far from harmonious. Inevitably, opposing Essences clash, by their nature attempting to strengthen and enforce their respective Essence upon the entire cosmos. In the planes of Soul, the essences of Good and Evil constantly war, attempting to guide other cosmic forces towards their respective conceptual philosophies. At the universe's Mind, ideas of unity and madness clash, each trying to guide the cosmos's mentality. In the Body planes, storms of Elements fight to dominate the physical world, while in Spirit, Life and Death each push the universe towards eternal youth or eternal silence.  

Still, it is the combination of all of these, the dichotomy between the perfect order of the four Components and the unyielding chaos of their constant clashes, that makes The Great Cosmic Wheel. Creatures are born, manifesting a Body of the physical; Their Minds given the ability to think, their Souls given the ability to feel, and finally the entire machine is powered with the spark of Spirit. Their entire existence feeds the wheel, as concepts they make feed back the Soul, and thoughts they think feed back the Mind, and as they die, they once again feed the Body and the Spirit, which in turn would feed off of each other. The Great Wheel turns, and as long as the four Components are in balance it would continue to turn forever.  

### Gods
An undeniable part of cosmos, the **Gods** are divine power manifested. Something between beings and forces of nature, these deities have incredible power, and are usually able to grant some of this power to other beings. Each god is unique, and strongly tied to a specific Essence, plane, or domains. Though their goals are unknowable by mortal standards, deities usually try and spread or strengthen their respective Essences, by teaching their philosophies to followers or fighting back opposing Essences. Indeed, a great amount of Essence is required to create a deity, but Essence alone is not enough - something else is required. A Spark of divinity. It is unknown whether this Spark is a miraculous combination of all four Components, or perhaps some primal piece of the universe that is even more cosmic than any of the four - whatever it is, the gods themselves would not share with mortals, and the ways to obtain it are rare and dangerous.

### Magic
Though planar Essence manifests in many ways around the cosmos - as places, ideas, creatures and gods - one of the most unique ways it may manifest is as **Magic**. A general term for Essences flowing together, Magic can be harnessed by denizens of the planes to achieve great miracles. Beings of the cosmos can control magic using learned skill, attunement to the Planes, or simply being born with a powerful connection to the Essences.  

Like the universe itself, scholars split Magic into four **Traditions**. **Arcane Magic** combines Body and Mind Essences, and allows one to exert control and logic over the universal forces to cast spells. **Divine Magic**, which is often associated with the Gods and their power, combines Soul and Spirit Essences, and is steeped in faith, the unseen, and belief in power source from the cosmos itself. **Primal Magic** is the intersection of Body and Spirit, linked to nature and instinct, sometimes considered the spirit of the world itself. Finally, **Occult Magic**, combining Mind and Soul, is rooted in mysteries and essoterica, and accessing it requires knowledge of the explainable and understanding of the ephemeral.  

# Planes of Body
The planes of **Body**, also known as the **Physical Planes** or **Inner Planes**, represent the physical universe. They are the very bones, fat and skin of the cosmos, the physical matter which gives shape and function to all things. Body Essence is made of **Moti**, the smallest building block of matter, which are Quintessence given physicality.   

The Physical Planes are unique, as they are the main place in the universe where **Mortals** can be found - the material plane is most suitable for mortal life to thrive. Though lowly in the grand scheme of the universe, mortals are nevertheless perhaps the closest to being a manifestation of the cosmos itself - combining many Essences inside them. Thus, even a Mortal can become a force of great change in the universe.  

## The Material Plane
**The Material Plane**, which is known by mortals simply as **The Universe** or **The World**, is the axis of the Body Planes, and perhaps of the cosmos itself. It's diversity, which stems from a blend of concepts and creations from all other planes, manifests in it's basic physicality - the plane is infinite in scale and variety, with galaxies containing countless starts and planets. These **Worlds** contain many different biomes, lands, and Mortal lifeforms who make the Plane their home -from humans and elves to animals and monsters and everything in-between.  The infinite worlds are separated by a vast void known as **The Dark Tapestry**, where nothing but the most eldritch aberrations lurk. Worlds of The Material Plane include:
- [[The World of Golarion|Golarion]]  

## The Elemental Planes
Formed out of raw, fundamental matter, the **Elemental Planes** serve as the physical building blocks of the cosmos. They produce elemental energy, the basic skeleton upon which the universe is built. Their natives are the **Elementals**, creatures made from such elemental energies. There are many Elemental Planes, including the "main" elements found across the universe but also many sub-elements or elemental combinations.
- Plane of Fire
- Plane of Water
- Plane of Earth
- Plane of Air
- Plane of Wood
- Plane of Metal

# Planes of Soul
The planes of **Soul**, also known as the **Outer Planes**, represent the metaphysical universe, the ideology and philosophy that guides it forward. Each is strongly aligned with a type of that ideology, a direction on the moral compass in which the plane attempts to guide the entire cosmos to step. Many of the Gods make their home here, feeling comfortable among the domains they represent. The Outer Planes are made of **Animus**, Quintessence built as soul matter. Souls of all beings are made from Animus, their views and life experiences coalescing into this pure soul-thread. When they die, a mortal's soul goes to the Plane most aligned with it's Animus, breaking it into the Plane's base matter to then once again spread across the cosmos.  Each Outer Plane is an infinite expanse of Animus, further divided into **Layers**. Each Layer is it's own bound metaphysical realm of it's plane, akin to a Material Plane world, and is often controlled by a Deity or a similarly powerful being.

## Upper Planes
The **Upper Planes**, also known as **Celestial Planes** or **Holy Planes**, are associated with goodly philosophies. Their inhabitants, the **Celestials**, work tirelessly to spread good and holiness throughout the universe. These Planes include:
- Heaven
- Nirvana
- Elysium
- Arcadia
- Bytopia
- Arborea
- Beastlands

## Liminal Planes
The **Liminal Planes**, also known as **Neutrality Planes** or **Planes of Balance**, are called so for not being strongly aligned with either Good or Evil, rather being associated with neutrality. They do exert other philosophies, such as the eternal struggle between Order and Chaos. Their inhabitant are known as **Monitors**, and are as diverse as the Liminal Planes themselves. These planes include:
- Axis
- Mechanus
- Acheron
- The Boneyard
- Ysgard
- The Malestorm
- Limbo

## Lower Planes
The **Lower Planes**, also known as the **Fiendish Planes** or **Unholy Planes**, are associated with evil philosophies. Their inhabitants, the **Fiends**, spread Evil and darkness through nefarious means across the cosmos. These planes include:
- Hell
- Gehenna
- Hades
- Abbadon
- Carceri
- The Abyss
- Pandemonium

# Planes of Mind
The Planes of **Mind**, also known as the **Cognitive Planes**, represent the thoughts of the universe. It is the shared psyche of the cosmos as a whole, it's brain and cognition. Their inhabitants are known collectively as **Psykers**, and are known for their great mental capacities which surpass any Mortal's. The base of the Cognitive Planes is called **Axiom**, Quintessence further refined to be thought in it's purest form. Whenever a mortal thinks, they generate more Axiom, and especially powerful beings know how to control the universe's Axiom in order to bend the cosmos around them to their will. Each Cognitive Plane is as infinite as the brain's capacity for ideas and imagination, and is further divided into **Cognitive Scapes**. Unlike Layers of the Soul Planes, which one can imagine as many rooms of the same house, Cognitive Scapes are like a long corridor of ever-shifting doors, each Scape going further and further into the subconscious back of the Plane's mind.  

## Planes of Logic
The **Planes of Logic** align with the rational thought, the clear idea, the collected memory, and the precise knowledge. They are where jumbled ideas become categorized sense, where incoherent thoughts become an organized mind-map. These planes include:
- The Spire
- Daanvi
- Shadesmar

## Planes of Imagination
The **Planes of Imagination** align with creativity, dreams and hallucinations. They represent the cosmos's ability to imagine and create, the infinite possibilities of the conscious and unconscious mind. These planes include:
- The Dreamfold
- Thelanis
- Kythri

## Planes of Madness
The **Planes of Madness** align with the unknowable, nightmares, and the breaking of the mind. They are the conscious taken to the extreme, the unimaginable given quaint form, insanity incarnate. Some claim the Planes of Madness should not exist, that they are an alien thought injected into the Mind of the cosmos from some far off, sinister place. Yet some claim that madness is a natural part of any psyche, and one must embrace it instead of fighting it. These planes include:
- The Far Realm
- Leng
- Xoriat

# Planes of Spirit
The **Planes of Spirit**, also known as the **Cosmic Spark**, are the universe's will, it's power and energy. They are Life and Death, Vitality and Void - unlike what many would believe, these are not opposite forces, but rather the same concept, the sum of two propagating wavelengths. The Essence of Spirit is **Ether**, Quintessence given a spark of emotion and vitality. Each of the Spirit Planes is an infinite, spiritual field, which is divided into **Realms**. Spirit Realms are like bubbles, constantly floating and merging with each other to spark new Realms.  

## The Core Planes
The **Core Planes**, also known as **Those At Creation's Edges**, are the ultimate metaphysical start and end of the cosmos - Life and Death. These are represented by the two Planes who generate pure Vitality and Entropy energies into the universe - Creation's Forge and The Void.
- Creation's Forge
- The Void

## The Echo Planes
The **Echo Planes**, also known as **Creation's Mirrors**, are the many echos of the cosmos's Spirit, representing that very Spirit seeping into the other Planes, giving spark and flame to bodies, ideas, and souls. Thus, these Planes inhabitants, although still uniquely spiritual, are often very intertwined with the other Planes, spreading their Spirit throughout the realms. These Planes include:
- Feyrealm
- Netherrealm
- Otherrealm


# Other Planes
Some Planes of the cosmos exist outside the regular division of the four Components, acting as a casing of the other Planes, a bridge between them, a fragment of them, or something else completely.  

## Transitive Planes
The **Transitive Planes** act as an echo of the universe, overlapping it and allowing creatures to pass over or between the Planes.
- Ethereal Plane
- Astral Plane

## Dimensions
**Dimensions** encase the cosmos in a different level of existence, foreign to mortal understanding. Though all beings interact with different dimensions all the time - while dreaming, or as the time passes, for example - that interaction almost never involves direct access. Those who manage to access a Dimensions or it's power are usually never seen again.
- Dimension of Time
- Dimension of Dreams
- Mirror Dimension

## Demiplanes
**Demiplanes** are smaller then regular Planes - usually finite sized. They are created when pieces of reality are fragmented and fused together, usually by a powerful being or a cosmic fluke, to create a stable new Plane. These float in the metaphysical space between the planes they were made from, and may serve as homes for demigods, laboratories for arch-wizards, or prisons for ancient monstrosities. Known Demiplanes include:
- Runeforge
